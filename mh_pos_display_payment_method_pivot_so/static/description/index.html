POS Display Payment Method in SO Pivot Report (Odoo17)

Boost your Point of Sale (POS) reporting capabilities in Odoo with our POS Payment Method Reporting Extension. This app adds payment method details to your POS order reports, providing you with deeper insights and more comprehensive analytics. With our app, you can easily view, analyze, and group your POS transactions by payment method, making it simpler to understand your sales performance and customer payment preferences.
