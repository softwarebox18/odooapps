Multi Company Favicon

Multi Company Favicon - Customizing favicons for individual companies in Odoo 17 is not currently supported. However, it's possible only for the website. We offer a solution that allows you to customize your own favicon for multiple companies. Simply navigate to the company settings, upload your favicon image, and you're all set up.

Configuration

  • Go to the Users & Company Menu

  • Open Company Form

  • Replace or Choose the Company Favicon

  • Perform the Same Steps for Multi Companies
